Our History

Two decades of institutional excellence.

2001

Foundation

Novus Ordo was founded in New York with a single mandate: to provide institutional-grade financial advisory to companies overlooked by the largest banks.

2008

Navigating the Crisis

During the global financial crisis, our disciplined risk frameworks protected clients and attracted new mandates from companies seeking guidance in turbulent markets.

2014

Global Expansion

Opening of offices in London, Singapore and São Paulo. Novus Ordo became a truly global firm with deep local expertise across three continents.

2019

AI Roadshow Platform Launch

Launched our proprietary investor matching technology — reducing roadshow cycles by 40% and increasing institutional allocation precision across all mandates.

Today

Market Leadership

Over $120B under management, 9,000+ completed transactions and a 96% client satisfaction rate. Recognized as the world's leading investment bank for complex transactions.
