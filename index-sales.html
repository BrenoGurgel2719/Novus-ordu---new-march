Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you
need to know

We answer the most common questions from those considering an institutional partnership with Novus Ordo.

Still have questions? Talk to us →

Novus Ordo exclusively serves legal entities — mid to large-sized companies seeking to structure financial market operations. We work with privately held companies in expansion phases, corporate groups exploring M&A, and corporations requiring structured capital raising. We do not serve individuals or early-stage startups without recurring revenue.

Our compensation is structured as a success fee — fully aligned with your results. We charge a success fee on the volume raised or the transaction value, with no fixed monthly retainers. For ongoing strategic advisory, a monthly retainer proportional to the scope applies. Exact figures are defined in the commercial proposal after the initial diagnosis, which is free and non-binding.

A typical Merger & Acquisition transaction takes between 4 and 12 months, depending on deal complexity, due diligence, regulatory approvals and negotiation between parties. Cross-border transactions may extend this timeframe. Our process is divided into clear phases: Diagnosis → Structuring → Roadshow → Negotiation → Closing, with bi-weekly progress reports for full transparency.

Onboarding begins with a free diagnostic meeting (in-person or by video call) where our partners understand your company's current stage, goals and challenges. Within 5 business days, we deliver a detailed proposal with scope, timeline and fee structure. After contract signing, we assign a dedicated Managing Director to your account with direct access through the Novus platform.
